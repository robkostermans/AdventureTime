Hidden Treasures

Scattered throughout the forest are remnants of an ancient civilization. Adventurers have reported finding Emerald Leaves and Moss Crystals that possess magical properties.

The path to the Mountain Peak begins at the northern edge of the forest, where the trees thin and rocky outcrops emerge. To the east, a sandy trail leads to the Golden Desert, while westward lies the Endless Ocean.

The Sacred Clearing

At the very center of the forest lies a clearing where no tree grows. Here, moonlight pools like liquid silver, and it is said that wishes made here have a way of coming true.

Many seek the Moonstone Pendant rumored to be buried beneath the clearing's center. Only those pure of heart may find it.
