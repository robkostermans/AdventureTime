The Crystal Caves

Deep within the mountain lie caves filled with Amethyst Clusters and Diamond Veins. The caves glow with an inner light, creating a spectacle that has inspired countless legends.

The southern slopes descend into the Enchanted Forest, while the eastern face overlooks the Golden Desert far below. To the west, a treacherous path leads down to the shores of the Endless Ocean.

The Observatory

At the very summit stands an ancient observatory, built by a civilization that could read the stars. The Star Charts within predict events across all realms, and the Celestial Compass can guide travelers anywhere in the world.

Treasures of the Heights

The mountain rewards the brave with rare minerals found nowhere else. Sky Iron forged from meteorites makes the finest weapons, while Cloud Crystals can capture and store lightning.

The ultimate prize is the Heart of the Mountain, a gem of pure starlight said to grant visions of the future. It rests in the deepest chamber of the Crystal Caves, protected by the Mountain Guardian.

The Eternal Flame

Despite the cold, a flame burns eternally at the Temple of Winds. This Sacred Flame has never been extinguished and is said to be a fragment of the sun itself. Pilgrims light torches from it to carry blessings back to their homes.

The monks offer shelter and wisdom to all who make the journey. In exchange, they ask only that travelers share stories of the lands below, adding to their vast collection of World Chronicles.
