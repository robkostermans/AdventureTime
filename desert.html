The Buried Temple

Deep beneath the largest dune lies the Temple of the Sun God. Its entrance appears only when the sun sets, casting long shadows that point the way. Within, adventurers have discovered Golden Scarabs and Amber Amulets of immense value.

The western edge of the desert gives way to the Enchanted Forest, while to the north, the sands gradually rise into the Mountain Peak. Southward, the dunes eventually meet the shores of the Endless Ocean.

The Mirage Market

Once every lunar cycle, a mysterious market appears in the heart of the desert. Merchants from distant lands gather to trade Rare Spices and Desert Silk. The market vanishes at dawn, leaving no trace but footprints in the sand.

Treasures of the Sands

The desert holds countless treasures for those brave enough to seek them. From Petrified Cactus with healing properties to Sand Pearls formed over millennia, the rewards match the risks.

Many adventurers seek the legendary Heart of the Desert, a ruby said to grant control over sandstorms. Its location remains one of the realm's greatest mysteries.
